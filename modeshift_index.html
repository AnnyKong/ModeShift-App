About the incident
A crash involving a tanker and two other vehicles happened in the southbound lanes of the I-5 collector-distributor lanes to I-90.
I-5 South/North
All lanes between James Street and West Seattle Bridge are closed. All traffic can use any of the HOV-ramps to exit.
I-90 East/West
All lanes between Rainier Avenue South and stadiums are closed.
Avoid travel in the area if possible
Currently no estimate for reopening but expect a long-term closure.
