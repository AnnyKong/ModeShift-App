Bringing Parents and Babies Together

THE PROBLEM

Parents often feel guilty about missing out on raising their baby and experiencing key development milestones post parental leave.


Research shows that roughly one one in seven women and one in ten men experience depression and guilt post parental leave.



How might we reduce the emotional disconnect between parents and their children post parental leave?

THE SOLUTION

A mobile and voice assistant based application which allows parents to connect with their babies while they are at work.


Parents can immersively interact with their babies through video chatting as well as engage in activities such as reading a book and singing a song.

Parents can also track developmental milestones through features such as automatic milestone detection, talking to Ask Nansee, the voice assistant, and capturing memories of their babies.

Babies can interact with their parents through an Amazon Alexa Spot-like device as well as engage in meaningful activites such as reading a book and singing a song.
